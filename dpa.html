Data Processing Addendum

Last updated: July 2026 · KVKK & GDPR-aligned

The core point. Conarium is self-hosted: it runs entirely inside your own infrastructure. In the product path, the vendor does not receive, access, store or process your data or your customers' personal data. You remain the sole controller and processor of your own data. There is, by design, nothing for us to mishandle.

This addendum sets out how personal data is handled between you ("Customer", the controller) and Emek Can Doğru, trading as Conarium ("Conarium") in connection with the website, the design-partner pilot and support. It is aligned with Turkey's KVKK (Law No. 6698) and the EU GDPR. For a signed, engagement-specific agreement, contact e.dogru@conarium.dev.

1. Roles

  • Product / self-hosted software: Conarium acts on data only within your environment, under your control. Conarium the vendor is not a processor of that data — it never leaves your perimeter and we never see it.
  • Website & support: for the limited personal data you share directly (a waitlist email, chat or support messages), Conarium acts as a processor/controller as described in the Privacy Policy.

2. Scope and purpose of processing

The only personal data Conarium processes is what you voluntarily send us: your contact email (to arrange access/pilots) and the content of your messages (to respond and support you). No customer database content, no end-customer PII, ever reaches Conarium.

3. Sub-processors

For the website/support processing only:

  • Vercel — website hosting.
  • Supabase — storage of the waitlist email.
  • An LLM provider — generating Concierge chat replies (message content only).

Because the product is self-hosted, there are no product sub-processors — your data does not flow to any third party through Conarium.

4. Security measures

  • The product enforces read-only access, fail-closed policy checks, PII masking before the model, and a tamper-evident, PII-safe audit log — all inside your environment (see the technical docs).
  • The website transmits over TLS, restricts source files from being served, and sets standard security headers.
  • Security disclosures: security.txt.

5. International transfers

Product data stays wherever you host it — it never transfers to us. For the limited website data, our sub-processors may operate outside your country; we rely on their standard contractual safeguards.

6. Data-subject requests & breach

For product data, you handle data-subject requests directly, since only you hold it. For website/support data, email e.dogru@conarium.dev and we will assist promptly. We will notify you without undue delay of any breach affecting personal data you shared with us.

7. Term

This addendum applies for as long as we process any personal data you have shared. On request we will delete or return it.
