Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version. Conarium the product is self-hosted — it runs entirely inside your own infrastructure. We never receive, see, or store your data or your customers' data. This policy therefore covers only two things: our website (conarium.dev) and our direct communications with you.

Who we are

This site and service are operated by Emek Can Doğru, trading as Conarium (conarium.dev), the data controller for the limited processing described below. Contact: e.dogru@conarium.dev.

What we collect, and why

  • Early-access / waitlist email. If you submit your email, we store it to contact you about access and pilots. Legal basis: your consent.
  • Messages you send us — the on-site "Concierge" chat and any email. Chat messages are sent to a third-party large-language-model provider solely to generate a reply; they are not used to train models. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in answering you.
  • Basic technical logs from our hosting provider (e.g. IP address, request time) for security and reliability. Legal basis: legitimate interest.
  • Fonts. We load Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address to serve the font files.

We do not use advertising trackers, we do not sell or rent your data, and we do not profile you.

The interactive demo

The "See what your AI sees" demo runs entirely in your browser. Anything you paste into it is processed locally and is never uploaded to us or anyone else.

Who else processes this data (sub-processors)

  • Vercel — website hosting.
  • Supabase — stores the waitlist email you submit.
  • An LLM provider — generates replies in the Concierge chat (messages only; no product data).

How long we keep it

Waitlist emails: until you ask us to delete them or we wind down the early-access programme. Chat/email: as long as needed to answer and follow up. Logs: a short rolling window set by our host.

Your rights

Under KVKK (Turkey) and the GDPR (EU) you can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to stop processing it. Email e.dogru@conarium.dev and we'll act on it — the founder handles these directly.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above changes with it. Material changes to how we use waitlist data will be emailed to you.
